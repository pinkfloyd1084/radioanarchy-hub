TRANSMISSION INCOMING: OUR MISSION

Radio Anarchy isn't just a destination—it is a Community of Communities. We are a unified digital network built from the ground up for players, server owners, admins, content creators, and mod makers.

Membership is 100% free. Securing a hub account grants you immediate access to the interactive Lounges, deep-dive tech Guides, our expanding Mod Repositories, Video Tutorials, the massive Retro Vault, and more.

This network grows with your help and becomes stronger through collaboration. Run your own community? Submit it to the hub and join a larger family of servers. Content creator? Broadcast your stream schedule. Mod maker? Advertise your work and link your GitHub. Curating a classic archive? Submit your Retro Game links.

Look around. Make friends. Have fun. Enjoy yourselves.

SERVER WIPE PROTOCOLS

At Radio Anarchy, our primary servers undergo a full wipe every 4 months (3 times a year) to keep the economy fresh and performance optimal for all players.

Other affiliated sister servers on the network maintain their own independent wipe schedules. Whenever they provide that intelligence, we will broadcast it directly to this terminal so you are always prepared.

NETWORK REGULATIONS

Below are the standard Discord regulations required to keep the comms clean and the gameplay fair:

  • [1] RESPECT THE COMM: Keep it civil. No racism, sexism, homophobia, or extreme toxicity. Banter is fine; being a menace is not.
  • [2] NO UNAUTHORIZED BROADCASTS: Do not advertise other servers, discords, or self-promote without clearance from High Command.
  • [3] NEUTRAL ZONES: Keep politics and real-world controversies out of the general channels. We are here to survive the apocalypse, not debate it.
  • [4] CHAIN OF COMMAND: Admin decisions are final. If you have a dispute, do not argue in global chat—open a private ticket.
